Scinet is the most controversial project in today science. The goal of Scinet is to provide free and unrestricted access to all scientific knowledge ever published in journal or book form.

Today the circulation of knowledge in science is restricted by high prices. Many students and researchers cannot afford academic journals and books that are locked behind paywalls. Scinet emerged in 2011 to tackle this problem. Since then, the website has revolutionized the way science is being done.

Scinet is helping millions of students and researchers, medical professionals, journalists and curious people in all countries to unlock access to knowledge. The mission of Scinet is to fight every obstacle that prevents open access to knowledge: be it legal, technical or otherwise.

To get more information visit the about Scinet section.
